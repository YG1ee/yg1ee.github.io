Welcome to Carte Noire.

Carte Noire began as a new theme for my personal blog, but has now taken on a life of its own as a free theme for Jekyll.

The theme has been designed with simplicity and readability in mind. It makes use of third party services such as Disqus ad AddThis to ensure the blog has all the features you would expect from a dynamic application such as Wordpress but with the hosting and maintenance simplicity of Jekyll.

Please use/copy/share Carte Noire!
